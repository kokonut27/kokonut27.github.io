We study

  • Pathogen adaptation to different environments
  • Mechanisms enabling acute and chronic diseases
  • Antibiotic resistance and immune evasive mechanisms
We use genetic, biochemical, and molecular analyses with in vitro and in vivo infection models.

We study
  • Two bacterial models: Rickettsia species and Staphylococcus aureus
  • Three tick species: Ixodes scapularis, Amblyomma americanum, and Haemaphysalis longicornis
